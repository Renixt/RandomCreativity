Privacy Policy

Last updated: 24/06/2024

Welcome to the Random Creativity website. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information.
This Privacy Policy explains how we use and protect any information that you give us when you use our website.

1. Information We Collect

When you use our website, we may access the following information from your Pinterest account: Your boards and pins.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we access from your Pinterest account solely to select random pins from any of your boards.
Specifically, we use your boards and pins to randomly select pins that we then show on the website, so you can use them to help your creative process.

3. Data Storage and Security

We do not store any data accessed from your Pinterest account.
All data is accessed in real-time through Pinterest's API and is used only for the duration of your session on our website.
We do not have any type of database that saves your data.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not share, sell, or distribute your personal information with any third parties.

5. Your Consent

By using our website, you consent to our Privacy Policy.

6. Changes to Our Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at renixt@hotmail.com.
